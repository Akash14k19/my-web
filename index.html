About Us

Our journey from humble beginnings to championship contenders

Founded in 2012, Team SygNitorS is a dedicated group of engineering enthusiasts committed to designing and racing high-performance go-karts. Our team blends innovation, precision, and teamwork to push the boundaries of karting technology.

With numerous awards in alignment, skidpad, and innovation we constantly strive for excellence. Beyond racing, we focus on knowledge sharing and mentoring aspiring engineers.

Join us on our journey to redefine motorsport engineering with passion and dedication!
